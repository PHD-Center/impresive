IMPRESIVE is an international multi-database study preparedness platform that enables timely evaluation of diseases and treatments through database standardization, integration, visualization, and distributed real-world evidence generation.

Core capabilities

From local data to decision-grade evidence

A connected pipeline that lets data from different countries answer the same question — without ever transferring raw patient-level records.

Database Standardization

Mapping different source structures to common variable definitions so one analytic program can run everywhere.

CDM Conversion

A common data model that keeps local governance intact while enabling consistent multi-country analysis.

Distributed Analytics

Raw data never leaves each country. We share code, diagnostics and aggregated results only.

Analysis Modules

Reusable, validated study designs — cohort, target-trial emulation, self-controlled, negative-control and more.

Evidence Integration

Not just a pooled estimate — understanding why results agree, differ, or cannot be compared across countries.

Results Visualization

Readiness dashboards, forest plots, trend and heterogeneity views that make findings comparable and clear.

Decision Translation

Turning evidence into something clinicians, regulators, HTA bodies and policymakers can act on — in time.
