Frequently asked questions

The questions we hear most often about how IMPRESIVE works.

Does IMPRESIVE collect each country's raw data?

No. Individual-level raw data stays in each country's local data center. IMPRESIVE uses a distributed-analysis model — what is shared is the analytic code, data-quality reports, aggregated statistical results and visualizations.

How is IMPRESIVE different from a typical multi-database study?

IMPRESIVE is not just a multi-database study. It is an integrated platform that emphasizes research preparedness, database standardization, integrated analysis workflows, results visualization, distributed analysis, reproducibility, transportability and decision interpretation.

What kind of topics are suitable for IMPRESIVE?

Topics that need cross-national comparison, rare-event research, extrapolation / transportability assessment, post-marketing surveillance, disease-burden evaluation, treatment-pattern analysis, or clinical and policy decision support.

Can IMPRESIVE collaborate with industry?

Yes — with clear governance, scientific review, transparent methods, conflict-of-interest disclosure and a defined publication policy.

Can students or early-career researchers join?

Yes. Students and early-career researchers can take part through the training program, the simulation lab, analysis-module development, dashboard development, or a concrete research project.
