The trust model

No raw patient-level data ever leaves its country

IMPRESIVE runs on distributed analysis. Each partner keeps individual-level data under local governance. Only the common protocol, standardized analytic code, data-quality diagnostics and aggregated results are shared — making international collaboration possible while protecting privacy.
