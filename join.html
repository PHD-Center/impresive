Join the IMPRESIVE network

Three clear pathways — whether you hold data, design studies, or make decisions.

Data partners

Data partners

Suitable for national claims databases, hospital EHR databases, disease registries, biobank-linked databases and research data centers.

Participation criteria

  • A clear data-governance mechanism
  • Ability to run local analysis
  • Ability to follow a common protocol
  • Willingness to share aggregated results
  • A local PI or data custodian
  • Willingness to take part in standardization, integration and visualization
Researchers

Researchers

Suitable for pharmacoepidemiologists, clinician-scientists, statisticians, data scientists, visualization experts, graduate students and postdoctoral researchers.

Ways to take part

  • Study proposal
  • Protocol writing
  • Phenotype development
  • Analysis module development
  • Dashboard design
  • Result interpretation
  • Manuscript writing
Decision partners

Industry / regulatory / policy

Suitable for pharmaceutical companies, regulatory agencies, HTA bodies, public-health agencies and international research networks.

Directions for collaboration

  • Disease burden evaluation
  • Drug safety evaluation
  • Comparative effectiveness research
  • Post-marketing surveillance
  • Treatment pattern assessment
  • Transportability assessment
  • Methodological consultation
  • Decision-grade RWE framework
